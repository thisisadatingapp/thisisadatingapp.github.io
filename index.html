How it works

Finding your gut feeling

RRHEA is built from the ground up for people who live life on a different schedule — one dictated, in part, by their digestive system. No judgment. No explanations needed.

01

Set Your Level

Choose your severity level honestly — from occasional sensitivity to daily adventures. Your matches will understand exactly where you're at.

02

Build Your Profile

Upload photos, write a bio, and share your story — or as much as you're comfortable with. The community is judgment-free.

03

Add Your Gut Tags

Select the conditions that shape your daily life — IBS, Crohn's, GERD, and more. Your Gut Tags appear right on your profile card, so common ground is established before the first message is even sent.

04

Discover & Swipe

Browse profiles filtered by distance, level, and compatibility. Like, pass, or send a Super Match to someone who really speaks to you.

05

Connect with Confidence

When you both like each other — it's a match. Chat with icebreakers built specifically for this community. No awkward first messages required.

Severity levels

We meet you where you are

Eight levels of digestive experience, so you always match with someone who truly understands your daily reality.

Level 0 — Minimal
Barely noticeable. The occasional off day. Most people wouldn't even call it a condition.
Level 1 — Mild
Occasional sensitivity. Mostly predictable. You know your triggers and plan around them.
Level 2 — Moderate
Flare-ups a few times a week. Bathroom awareness is part of your everyday planning.
Level 3 — Active
Daily management required. You've memorized every restroom on your commute.
Level 4 — Serious
Significant impact on daily life. You're a pro at navigating the world strategically.
Level 5 — Severe
Constant vigilance. Spontaneity requires serious planning. Others rarely understand.
Level 6 — Extreme
Life-altering daily challenge. You need a partner, not a person who needs explaining to.
Level 7 — Critical
Every day is a negotiation. You deserve someone who shows up fully — no questions asked.
