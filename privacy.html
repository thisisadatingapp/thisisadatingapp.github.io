Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2026
Last Updated: July 2, 2026

Welcome to the RRHEA Privacy Policy. At RRHEA, we believe every connection should start with respect and honesty — especially when it comes to your personal information. This Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the RRHEA mobile application ("App") and our website at rrhea-app.com ("Site").

When we say "RRHEA," "we," "us," or "our," we mean RRHEA App LLC, the company that operates the RRHEA dating app. By using our App or Site, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Have questions about this Policy? Contact us at support@rrhea-app.com.

We may update this Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available at rrhea-app.com/privacy-policy. We will notify you of any material changes by email or through the App.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of the App.

1.1 Registration Information

When you create a RRHEA account, we collect:

  • Name and username
  • Email address
  • Password (securely hashed — we never store it in plain text)
  • Age
  • Gender and sexual orientation
  • Location (city or GPS coordinates)
  • Height

1.2 Profile Information

Information you choose to add to your profile, including:

  • Biography
  • Photographs (up to 6)
  • Severity level (L0–L7)
  • Gut Tags (e.g., IBS, Crohn's, Ulcerative Colitis, Lactose Intolerance, Celiac, etc.)

1.3 Social Login Information

If you sign in with Apple, Google, or Facebook, we receive limited information from these providers:

  • Apple: Your Apple login, a name (which you can edit), and an email address. You can choose to use your personal email or Apple's private relay email.
  • Google: Your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Facebook: Your name, email address, and profile picture.

We use this information only to create and authenticate your RRHEA account.

1.4 Purchase Information

If you purchase Premium, Profile Boosts, Super Likes, or merchandise, we record:

  • The type of purchase (e.g., premium, boost, super_likes)
  • The purchase amount
  • The date of purchase

All payments are processed through PayPal. RRHEA never sees, collects, or stores your payment card details, bank account information, or PayPal password. Merchandise purchases are processed through Shopify. See Section 7 for details on third-party services.

1.5 Messages

When you chat with matches, your messages are stored securely in our database to enable real-time messaging and message history across devices.

1.6 Geolocation Information

If you grant RRHEA location permission on your device, we collect your GPS coordinates to:

  • Show you profiles of people near you
  • Calculate and display distances between you and other members
  • Enable location-based filtering

If you prefer not to share GPS, you can manually enter a city or zip code instead.

You can turn off location access at any time: iPhone — Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > RRHEA

1.7 Device Information

We collect basic device information to ensure the App functions properly, including your device model and operating system version.

1.8 Photo Information

Photos you upload are screened on your device using Apple's Vision framework for prohibited content before being accepted. This check happens entirely on your iPhone — no photos are sent to external servers for moderation. Accepted photos are stored in your profile and synced to the cloud.

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the RRHEA dating service
  • Create and maintain your account and profile
  • Show your profile to other members based on compatibility, location, and preferences
  • Enable matching and messaging between mutual likes
  • Process purchases (Premium subscriptions, Boosts, Super Likes)
  • Sync your data across devices so you can log in from any iPhone
  • Enforce our Terms and Conditions and Community Guidelines
  • Protect the safety and security of our members
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve the App based on usage patterns

2.1 Matching and Filtering

RRHEA uses your profile information to show you compatible profiles. Factors include:

  • Your sexual orientation and gender preferences
  • Your location and distance preferences
  • Severity level filters you apply
  • Age range filters you apply

2.2 Moderation

We use on-device photo screening to prevent prohibited content from being uploaded. We also review reports submitted by members and may suspend or ban accounts that violate our Community Guidelines.

3. What Information Can Others See?

When you use RRHEA, other members can see the information you put on your profile, including:

  • Your name, age, and photos
  • Your bio
  • Your severity level and Gut Tags
  • Your city (not exact coordinates)
  • Your height, gender, and orientation

We encourage you to think carefully about what you share. Do not put sensitive information on your profile such as your full address, phone number, email address, financial details, or government ID numbers.

Messages are only visible to you and the person you are chatting with.

4. How Do We Protect Your Information?

4.1 Data Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted open-source backend platform. All data is:

  • Encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS
  • Protected by row-level security policies that ensure users can only access their own data
  • Passwords are securely hashed — never stored in plain text

4.2 Sensitive Health Information

Your severity level and Gut Tags are considered sensitive health data. Providing them is entirely optional and voluntary — you can create a profile and use RRHEA without them. We use this information only to match you with compatible profiles and to power your discovery filters; we do not use it for any other purpose.

You can edit or remove your severity level and Gut Tags at any time from your profile (Edit Profile), and removing them stops their use for matching going forward. Where applicable law requires it, we process this sensitive data on the basis of your explicit consent, which you provide by choosing to add it and which you can withdraw at any time by removing it.

We treat this information with the utmost care. It will never be sold, shared with advertisers, or disclosed to health insurers or any other third party.

4.3 Authentication Security

Account authentication is handled by Supabase Auth, which supports industry-standard security practices including secure token management and OAuth 2.0 for social logins.

5. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide our services.

  • Profile data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted when you deactivate your account.
  • Messages: Retained while both users' accounts are active. Deleted when either user deactivates.
  • Purchase records: Retained for 6 years after account deletion for tax and audit purposes.
  • Reports and blocks: Retained for the duration needed to enforce community safety.

When you deactivate your account, your profile, matches, and messages are permanently deleted. Purchase records are retained only as required for legal and financial obligations.

Account deletion may take a reasonable period of time to complete across our active systems and routine backups. We may retain certain information after deletion where necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, or maintain records relating to fraud, safety, and security. Any such retained data is kept only for as long as legally necessary and is then deleted.

6. Legal Bases for Processing (EU/UK GDPR)

If you are in the European Union or United Kingdom, we rely on the following legal bases under the GDPR to process your personal data:

  • Performance of a contract: to create your account and provide the RRHEA service you request — matching, messaging, and related features.
  • Consent: for optional information you choose to share, including precise GPS location and any health-related details (such as gut conditions). You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests: to keep RRHEA safe and reliable — security, fraud prevention, and content moderation — balanced against your rights and freedoms.
  • Compliance with legal obligations: to meet requirements such as tax and accounting recordkeeping for purchases.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information by editing your profile
  • Delete your account and all associated data (Settings > Account Actions > Deactivate Account)
  • Withdraw consent for location sharing at any time through your device settings
  • Opt out of marketing communications

For users in the European Union and United Kingdom, you have additional rights under the GDPR including the right to data portability, the right to restrict processing, and the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests.

For users in California, you have rights under the CCPA including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. RRHEA does not sell your personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@rrhea-app.com.

8. Third-Party Services

RRHEA uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Authentication, database storage, and real-time messaging. Supabase processes and stores your account data, profile information, matches, and messages.
  • PayPal — Payment processing for in-app purchases (Premium, Boosts, Super Likes). RRHEA never receives or stores your payment card details.
  • Shopify — Merchandise sales through our online store.
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — Location geocoding (converting city names or zip codes to coordinates). Only the city name or zip code is transmitted — no personal information.
  • Giphy — GIF content in chat messages, loaded via public CDN URLs.
  • Apple, Google, Facebook — OAuth authentication for social login. Only your name and email are shared with RRHEA.

We do not use any tracking, analytics, or advertising services. We do not share your personal information with advertisers.

9. Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your personal information if we are required to do so by law, or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) comply with a legal obligation, court order, subpoena, or other valid legal process; (b) respond to a lawful request from a government agency or law enforcement; (c) protect and defend the rights, property, or safety of RRHEA, our users, or the public; or (d) detect, prevent, or investigate fraud, security issues, or violations of our Terms. Where permitted by law, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you before disclosing your information in response to such a request.

10. Children's Privacy

RRHEA is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we learn that we have collected information from a person under 18, we will delete it immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using RRHEA, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

12. Safety and Reporting

RRHEA has zero tolerance for harassment, body shaming, mockery of health conditions, unsolicited explicit content, or discrimination of any kind. You can:

  • Report a user by long-pressing a message or using the menu on their profile
  • Block a user at any time — they will be removed from your matches and prevented from seeing your profile
  • All reports are recorded and reviewed. Accounts with multiple violations will be suspended or permanently banned.

If you believe you or someone else is in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services (911 in the US).
